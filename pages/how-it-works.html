The Full Circle
L2B Enterprise Program (Pilot)

Practising learning in academic summers, we guide our students through the entire agricultural lifecycle.

The Preneur Agro Tech College (PATC) model is a pedagogical framework designed to bridge the gap between theoretical agronomy and scalable market enterprise.

The L2B Lifecycle

A Sequential Methodology

STAGE 01
LEARNING

Learning

Foundational study encompassing plant physiology, soil chemistry, and the economics of global food systems. Students establish a robust intellectual core and research methodology.

STAGE 02
PLANTING

Planting

The transition from theory to soil. Students implement precision seeding techniques using historical climate data and soil nutrient mapping.

STAGE 03
GROWING

Growing

Active management of the ecosystem. Students utilise automated sensing and intervention protocols to ensure optimal crop health and resilience.

STAGE 04
HARVESTING

Harvesting

Strategic timing and low-impact collection techniques designed to preserve nutritional density and minimise post-harvest loss.

STAGE 05
PROCESSING

Processing

Engineering value-add components. Raw produce is transformed into sophisticated agricultural products within our state-of-the-art facilities.

STAGE 06
PACKAGING

Packaging & Branding

Students develop trusted identities and professional brands that reflect the quality and integrity of their harvest.

STAGE 07
SCALING

Selling & Scaling

Market integration. Establishing supply chain channels to create the commercial viability of their enterprise.

Foundation

The Technology Layer

Technology at PATC is a discipline in isolation, but the infrastructure glue that ties all model sciences.

Precision Intelligence
A network of the best AI sensors providing geospatial density on soil hydration, pH, toxin levels, and atmospheric conditions.
Predictive Analysis
Machine learning protocols that analyse historical data and market trends to forecast demand with institutional accuracy.
Immutable Provenance
Block chain backed traceability systems that record the value chain of every product from the original seed to the final consumer purchase.
