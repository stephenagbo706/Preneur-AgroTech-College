Our Mission

To cultivate the next generation of agropreneurs through hands-on technology, sustainable practices, and innovative business models.

Our Vision

A food-secure Africa led by tech-empowered professionals who see agriculture as a high-growth, modern industry.

PATC aims to be among the first African institutions to run a truly full-cycle ecosystem—where we educate, produce, process, package, and take products to market, so learning doesn't end in the classroom or on the farm.
Mission

What We Exist To Do

Build an Africa-focused production and enterprise economy by training agrotech makers and business builders through a full-cycle model—from learning to market and growth.

Vision

Where We're Going

A future where learning reliably leads to enterprise—where young people can build productive, value-adding businesses that strengthen communities and local economies.

What "Agrotech" Means at PATC

Practical technology for real conditions

At PATC, agrotech means using practical technology to improve results across the value chain—better production discipline, stronger quality, lower waste, smarter packaging, and stronger sales systems. We train learners to use tools and systems that fit real conditions, not ideal conditions.
