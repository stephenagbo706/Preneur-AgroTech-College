The STEAAM Framework

PATC's STEAAM model integrates Science, Technology, Engineering, Agriculture, Arts, and Mathematics to build practical enterprise capability from production to market execution.

S

Science

Evidence-driven decisions for production and post-harvest quality.

T

Technology

Simple tools for data habits, tracking, and operational clarity.

E

Engineering

Systems thinking for repeatable routines and process improvements.

A

Agriculture

Practical farm execution tied directly to market outcomes.

A

Arts

Branding, storytelling, and product presentation that drive trust.

M

Mathematics

Pricing, costing, and margin control for sustainable enterprises.
